Decision rules

  • Did your team shoot / record it?   video → 02_Footage/01_Originals  ·  audio → 03_Audio/01_Production
  • Acquired media (bought or given)?   video → Stock / AI / Provided  ·  audio → Music / SFX / VO  ·  photo → 04_Graphics/03_Stills
  • Something you rendered?   client sees it → 00_Deliver  ·  internal only → 05_Intermediates
  • A graphic asset?   raw ingredient → 04_Graphics/01_Resources  ·  your export → 04_Graphics/02_Built
  • Paperwork or a Frame.io EDL?   → 06_Documents

Raw ingredients: logos, fonts, brand kits, reference images.

Graphics you created or exported (titles, lower-thirds, end cards).

Photos and frame grabs — including client or photographer photos.

+Client / photographer photos → 03_Stills
–Mood / reference images → 01_Resources
+Transcodes, comps / VFX, pulls, sizzle drafts — anything you can re-create from the project. Keep versions (v1, v2…).
–If the edit relinks to it and you can't re-create it, it's a source, not an Intermediate (e.g. cleaned audio → 03_Audio/05_Processed).
–Don't leave empty folders.
+Brief, script, transcripts, contracts, Frame.io review EDLs (comment markers).
