Stage 01

Project folder structure

Every project follows the same layout. Folders are organized by type of media, not by stage. Open any folder to see what goes inside.

Type in the filter to jump to a folder, or activate any folder to reveal what goes inside.

No match — try a format like .mov, or browse the folders below.

The folders

Approved deliverables — every format and resolution you hand off (16:9, vertical, square, social). A subtitle .srt sits next to the master it belongs to.

Review cuts sent for feedback before sign-off (v1, v2, fine cut).

–Internal-only renders → 05_Intermediates

Your Resolve project backup (.drp). The edit lives here.

Interchange files: DRT, XML, AAF, EDL.

Color LUTs (.cube) used in the grade.

–No media — footage and audio live in 02_Footage / 03_Audio

Footage your team shot. Each card/camera in its own folder; proxies inside that segment's Proxy.

Licensed stock clips.

AI-generated video.

Video the client or a third party gave you.

+Shot by your team → 01_Originals. Otherwise Stock / AI / Provided.
–Never rename 01_Originals — Resolve links by path.

Sound recorded on set / location: lav, boom, scratch.

Voiceover and narration.

Sound effects, ambience and Foley.

Music tracks (licensed or provided).

Raw ingredients: logos, fonts, brand kits, reference images.

Graphics you created or exported (titles, lower-thirds, end cards).

Photos and frame grabs — including client or photographer photos.

+Client / photographer photos → 03_Stills
–Mood / reference images → 01_Resources
+Transcodes, comps / VFX, pulls, sizzle drafts. Keep versions (v1, v2…).
–Don't leave empty folders.
+Brief, script, transcripts, contracts, Frame.io review EDLs (comment markers).

Reference

  • Did your team shoot / record it?   video → 02_Footage/01_Originals  ·  audio → 03_Audio/01_Production
  • Acquired media (bought or given)?   video → Stock / AI / Provided  ·  audio → Music / SFX / VO  ·  photo → 04_Graphics/03_Stills
  • Something you rendered?   client sees it → 00_Deliver  ·  internal only → 05_Intermediates
  • A graphic asset?   raw ingredient → 04_Graphics/01_Resources  ·  your export → 04_Graphics/02_Built
  • Paperwork or a Frame.io EDL?   → 06_Documents
  • No spaces. Use _. Version with _v01, _v02, _v03 — that's how we track revisions.
    Example: KayleeGlatting_Highlight_v02.mov
