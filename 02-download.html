Stage 02

Download the material

Juan puts the proxies and audio on Frame.io. You download them and edit with the proxies — the originals stay with Juan.

How it works

  1. What you downloadProxies + audio — that's all you need. Not the originals; Juan keeps those.
  2. How you downloadFrame.io Drive (recommended) — mount the project and copy. Or the Frame.io web Download button.
  3. Where it goesKeep the folders as they are. Each proxy → its segment's 02_Footage/01_Originals/<segment>/Proxy; audio → 03_Audio/01_Production.

Remember

+Frame.io Drive is the fast lane — local cache, optimized transfer.
+You edit with proxies; Juan relinks to the originals at the end.
–Don't rename or move folders — Resolve relinks by path, and Frame.io mirrors them.
